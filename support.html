FAQ

The screen dims or sleeps. Keep "Keep screen awake" on in the overlay (swipe down / press select in a scene).

How do I change the sound mix? In any scene, press the touchpad (select) to open the overlay, then adjust each layer under Mix.

Is there a subscription? No — Hearth is a one-time purchase with no ads and no subscription.
