Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Hearth does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Hearth plays bundled ambient video and audio entirely on your device. It does not require an account, does not connect to any server for its core function, and does not transmit any information about you.

Purchases

In-app purchases are not used. The app is a one-time paid purchase handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your payment details.
